How It Started

```

I never imagined that a simple online conversation could leave such a lasting impact on me. But then I met you.

```

What I Admire About You

```

You're a traditional Telugu girl with values that are becoming rare to find. The respect you show, the way you think, and the warmth in your personality made me admire you deeply.

It wasn't something that happened in a day. Every conversation made me appreciate you a little more.

```

```

```
